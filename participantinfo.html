Study Participant Information Sheet

What is the purpose of this pilot study?

This trial study is being used to refine the methodology of the research project. The results from this study will be examined to test if the concepts being explored within the research thesis are feasible for a larger data collection. The information you provide in this questionnaire will be used for a Business and Management PhD. The study is examining the perception of imagery and social media usage within Higher Education.

Who can take part?

This study is open to:

  • UK Undergraduate students
  • Over 18 years of age

Do I have to take part?

This is completely voluntary; you are not obligated to take part if you do not want to. If you do want to take part please make sure you read everything provided to you, especially on this participant information sheet as it contains vital information about what you will be doing.

If, however, you decide that you no longer want to take part in this study after starting the questionnaire then do not proceed to submit if you have not completed it. You can exit out of the questionnaire at any time. If you have any questions please feel free to contact the researcher at any time (contact details provided below).

You are free to withdraw from the study at any time while completing it, if you change your mind and would not like to take part just simple exit out of the questionnaire, any incomplete responses will be deleted. You have the option to provide your email on the consent form, this is entirely optional, and you do not have to do this if you would like your submission to remain completely anonymous, however, if you do wish to withdraw your data your email will be needed to do this. Please keep this in mind, that this needs to be done by --- , after this date it is like that the data will have been input for analysis.

What am I expected to do?

You will be expected to complete this questionnaire. Please ensure to read each question carefully and respond as accurately as possible. This questionnaire should take no longer than 10 minutes to complete.

The questionnaire is split into 3 sections:

  • Section 1: contains demographical questions and the use of your social media accounts.
  • Section 2: contains the TIPI (Gosling, 2003) a standardised personality data measure and part of the STPS (Kaptien, 2012).
  • Section 3: The final section will contain a selection of images and you will be asked questions surrounding your interpretation of the images and the behaviour that these images will invoke.

Are there any risks to taking part?

There are no foreseeable risks to taking part in this study. The questionnaire will be kept anonymous and confidential. If, however at any point during the questionnaire you do feel distressed at any time you are not required to carry on completing the questionnaire. If you do have any issues please contact the researcher at any time. Will my information be kept confidential? All information you provide throughout this questionnaire will be kept confidential at all times; all information is anonymised in the dataset. All information will be stored on a secure system and password-protected computers. The only people who will have access to the data set will be the researcher and supervisors if necessary.
